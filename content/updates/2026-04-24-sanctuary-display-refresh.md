## What Changed

Replaced the front-of-house confidence monitors with updated widescreen panels. Lyrics and sermon notes now appear at a larger, easier-to-read font size during services.

## Why

The previous monitors were showing signs of burn-in and the resolution made small text difficult to read from the stage.

## Impact

- Worship team, speakers, and musicians using the stage monitors
- No changes to the projection screen or congregation-facing displays

## Notes

Reach out to the tech team if you notice calibration issues during rehearsal.
