## What Changed

Switched the livestream audio feed from a direct board mix to a dedicated mix routed through the digital console. The online mix can now be adjusted independently from the in-room sound.

## Why

Feedback from online congregation members indicated inconsistent audio quality, particularly when room acoustics introduced reverb that carried over into the stream.

## Impact

- Online viewers on YouTube and the church website
- Tech team volunteers operating the digital console during services

## Notes

The new routing is documented in the quick-reference binder at the tech booth. A short training walkthrough will be scheduled for the next volunteer orientation.
