## What Changed

Launched the redesigned church website with updated service times, an events calendar, and a new sermon archive with search. Mobile experience was a core focus throughout the rebuild.

## Why

The previous site was built on a platform that was reaching end-of-life and was difficult to update without developer access. The new site uses a modern CMS that any staff member can edit.

## Impact

- Entire congregation and visitors searching for service information online
- Staff members updating events and announcements
- First-time visitors finding contact and location details

## Notes

Staff training on the new CMS took place in March. If you need a refresher or have questions about editing content, contact the communications team.
